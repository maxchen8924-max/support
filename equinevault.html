Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track vaccinations and vet visits?

Add a health record for each horse with date, type (vaccination, deworming, vet visit, farrier, dental, etc.), notes, and photos. Set reminders so you never miss a scheduled visit.

How do I manage Coggins test records?

Add a Coggins test as a health record with the test date and result. Attach a photo of the certificate for quick access at shows or border crossings.

Can I generate reports for my vet or insurance?

Yes. EquineVault offers 3 PDF report templates designed for veterinarians, insurance companies, and potential buyers. Go to Settings > Export to generate.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Backup. This creates a file containing all your horses and health records. Share via AirDrop, email, or save to the Files app.

Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Yes. Create a backup on your old phone, transfer the file to your new phone, then restore. Photos stored locally are not included in the backup.

Does the app require internet?

No. EquineVault works 100% offline. Perfect for use at the barn, shows, or anywhere without cell signal.

How many horses can I manage?

The free version supports 1 horse with 90 days of visible history. Upgrade to Premium ($14.99 one-time purchase) for unlimited horses and full history access.

Is there a subscription?

No. EquineVault is free to download. The optional Premium upgrade is a one-time purchase of $14.99 — no subscriptions, no recurring fees, no ads.

I lost my data. Can you recover it?

Unfortunately, no. All data is stored locally on your device. We strongly recommend creating regular backups.
