Frequently Asked Questions

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data > JSON Backup. This creates a file containing all your vehicles and maintenance records. Share it via AirDrop, email, or save to the Files app.

Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Yes. Export a backup on your old phone, transfer the file to your new phone, then import it. Note: photos stored locally are not included in the backup file.

Does the app require internet?

No. Precision Ledger works 100% offline. No internet connection is needed for any feature.

What export formats are available?

PDF service reports, CSV spreadsheets, and JSON full-data backups.

Can I track multiple cars?

Yes. Add as many vehicles as you need. Each vehicle has its own service history, cost tracking, and records.

Is there a subscription?

No. Precision Ledger is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads.

I lost my data. Can you recover it?

Unfortunately, no. All data is stored locally on your device. We strongly recommend creating regular backups.
