Frequently Asked Questions

How are my GPS coordinates protected?

All GPS coordinates are encrypted with AES-256 encryption and stored locally on your device. Even if someone accesses your phone's file system, coordinates cannot be read without the app.

Does the app share my location data?

Never. Your spot locations never leave your device. When you export CSV or PDF reports, GPS coordinates are intentionally excluded to protect your secret spots.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Backup. This creates a file containing all your records. Share via AirDrop, email, or save to the Files app.

Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Yes. Create a backup on your old phone, transfer the file to your new phone, then restore. Photos stored locally are not included in the backup.

Does the app require internet?

No. HuntCache works 100% offline. No internet connection is needed for any feature, including GPS spot recording.

What export formats are available?

PDF and CSV reports for your harvest records. GPS coordinates are excluded from exports to protect your spots.

Is there a subscription?

No. HuntCache is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads.

I lost my data. Can you recover it?

Unfortunately, no. All data is stored locally and encrypted on your device. We strongly recommend creating regular backups.
