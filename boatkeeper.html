Frequently Asked Questions

How do I back up my data?

Go to More > Backup All Data. This creates a JSON file containing all your boats, maintenance logs, schedules, parts, fuel logs, and trips. You can share this file via AirDrop, email, or save it to the Files app.

How do I restore from a backup?

Go to More > Restore from Backup. The app will show your available backup files. Select one to restore. Your current data is automatically backed up before the restore, so you can always go back.

Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Yes. Create a backup on your old phone (More > Backup All Data), share the file to your new phone (via AirDrop, email, or iCloud Drive), then use Restore from Backup on the new phone. Note: photos are stored locally and are not included in the backup file.

Does the app require internet?

No. BoatKeeper Pro works 100% offline. No internet connection is needed for any feature. Your data never leaves your device.

How do I export a PDF service report?

Go to More > Export Data > PDF. Select the boat you want to export. The app generates a professional PDF report with maintenance history and photos that you can share for insurance claims, resale documentation, or marina inspections.

What currencies are supported?

USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, NZD, CAD, CHF, JPY, SGD, HKD, and more. Go to More > Currency to change your default currency.

Is there a subscription?

No. BoatKeeper Pro is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. Ever.

I lost my data. Can you recover it?

Unfortunately, no. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no servers or cloud storage, we cannot recover lost data. We strongly recommend creating regular backups via More > Backup All Data.
