Frequently Asked Questions

Does HiveKeep require an internet connection?

No. HiveKeep is designed for apiaries and bee yards where connectivity is unreliable. Every core feature works offline.

What can I track in the app?

You can record hive details, inspections, harvests, and treatments, including follow-up dates for recurring work such as varroa management.

Can I export my records?

Yes. HiveKeep can generate PDF exports so you can keep clean records for consultants, associations, or your own archive.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple system storage. We do not collect or transmit your data.

Can you recover my data if I delete the app?

No. Because HiveKeep keeps everything on your device, we do not have server-side backups or access to your records.
