Frequently Asked Questions

How does biometric security work?

IronVault uses Face ID or Touch ID to lock your collection. Your biometric data is handled entirely by iOS and never stored by our app.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Backup. This creates an encrypted backup file containing all your collection data. Share it via AirDrop, email, or save to the Files app.

Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Yes. Create a backup on your old phone, transfer the file to your new phone, then restore from backup. Photos stored locally are not included in the backup.

Does the app require internet?

No. IronVault works 100% offline. No internet connection is needed for any feature. Your data never leaves your device.

Can I track serial numbers?

Yes. Each item entry supports serial numbers, photos, purchase details, and maintenance history.

What export formats are available?

PDF inventory reports and CSV spreadsheets for insurance documentation or personal records.

Is there a subscription?

No. IronVault is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads.

I lost my data. Can you recover it?

Unfortunately, no. All data is stored locally on your device. We strongly recommend creating regular backups.
