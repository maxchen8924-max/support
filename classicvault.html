Frequently Asked Questions

How do I back up my collection?

Go to Settings > Backup. This creates a file containing all your vehicles, service history, and records. Share it via AirDrop, email, or save to the Files app.

Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Yes. Create a backup on your old phone, transfer the file to your new phone, then restore. Photos stored locally are not included in the backup file.

Does the app require internet?

No. ClassicVault works 100% offline. No internet connection is needed for any feature.

Can I track matching numbers?

Yes. Each vehicle supports engine number, chassis number, and matching status tracking (Original/Replaced/Unknown).

How do I export my collection?

ClassicVault supports HTML report exports for insurance documentation or personal records.

What currencies are supported?

Automatic currency display based on language. Supports $, ¥, €, ₩, NT$.

How many vehicles can I add?

No limit. All data is stored locally on your device.

Is there a subscription?

No. ClassicVault is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads.
